Research Project: WhatsApp Group Chat Analysis by Using Machine Learning

This project involves exporting chat data from a WhatsApp group to analyze the frequency of messages from each participant. The analysis identifies the most active members using word clouds and compares two algorithms: Decision Tree and Naive Bayesian.

This research project analyzes sentiments in Amazon book reviews. It is a multiclass classification problem, categorizing reviews into negative, positive, and neutral. The insights from this analysis help people choose books and aid in business development.
